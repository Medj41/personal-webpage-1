Technology

I’m also really passionate about tech, I am the tech ambassador at my current job and help colleagues set up their computers and answer any tech related questions. I have spent an entire weekend in front of my screen coding that’s how much I love it! One of the most exciting things I find about technology is its constant evolution and innovation. There are always new developments and advancements being made, which means there are always new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow. This means that a career in tech is never stagnant or boring, as there is always something new to explore and discover. In addition, the tech industry is known for its diverse and inclusive culture, which fosters creativity and collaboration. Working in tech often means being part of a team of highly motivated and talented individuals who share a passion for innovation and making a difference. This can lead to a sense of community and belonging that is hard to find in other fields.
